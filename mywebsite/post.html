Automating Decision-Making

This project I started when I was learning python through online courses and learned about Pandas library.

When the Russian invasion separated my best friend and me, we took up a tradition of having zoom calls and watching something together every night. Since my best friend is an anime lover, we agreed to watch anime. But the question "What anime are we watching today?" has sometimes been a problem.

So I decided to automatize the decision-making process to avoid some disagreements.
